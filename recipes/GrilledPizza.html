Grilled Pizza Bianca

Description

Prepared pizza dough allows the cook to grill a tasty pizza bianca, or white pizza, in under 30 minutes. Why call out for delivery when you can make an incredible pizza at home?

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 pound prepared pizza dough
  • 8 slices fresh mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds; stir in cream. Add 2/3 cup Parmesan cheese, and stir until smooth. Let Alfredo sauce cool to room temperature, about 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat a gas grill with all burners on High for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Divide pizza dough in half. Shape each portion of the pizza dough into an 8 to 10-inch disc. Reduce the flame on half the grill burners to Low, to create areas of direct and indirect heat.
  4. Carefully place discs of dough, one at a time, onto the grill over direct heat. Close the cover. Let dough grill until the bottom is just barely cooked and shows char marks, 1 to 3 minutes.
  5. Turn crust ninety degrees (do not flip dough over). Grill until crust shows light grill marks, 1 to 3 minutes.
  6. Move pizza dough to indirect heat. Spoon Alfredo sauce over dough. Top each crust with four slices mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle remaining 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese evenly over pizzas.
  7. Grill until cheese is melted, about 3 minutes. Carefully remove pizza from the grill.
